Terms and conditions for ustaz

  1. Must have the attitude to build a career in Hifz teaching.
  2. You must be a hardworking teacher who can participate in Quran competitions and win prizes.
  3. Must have permission from father or guardian
  4. We must adopt a modern and scientific teaching attitude.
  5. If there is a major crime committed during the past employment, special consideration will be given to those who have avoided it and repented, but if they commit it again, a lifetime ban will be imposed.
  6. By serving the Hifz Madrasa, one must not only become a practitioner themselves, but also have the attitude of making students a practitioner.
  7. The organization you will work for cannot be harmed in any way.
  8. Must have the mindset to serve anywhere in Bangladesh, although there is an opportunity for transfer.
  9. If you have any complaints or suggestions regarding the institution, you should inform Mudarrisul Quran Bangladesh in writing. Mudarrisul Quran will inform them of their decision within a maximum of 90 days.
  10. A maximum of two exemptions can be granted. If the exemption is granted for the first time, re-registration can be granted after a minimum of three years, but if the exemption is granted for the second time, registration cannot be granted for life.
  11. In case of transfer, application must be made at least 2 months in advance.
  12. Salary and allowances will be paid as per the pay scale.
  13. Registration must be renewed every year by paying the service charge on time.
  14. There are some activities that Mudarris ul Quran will implement in the future and they need to be agreed upon.
  15. In addition to our salary structure, there will be a grant scheme to support us in various disasters, so no one will be able to approach student guardians for any gifts or assistance.
  16. If an institution does not allow its own institution to operate next to it, its registration will be cancelled and the Mudarris Quran will not provide any teacher assistance to its institution.
  17. If you can successfully and with good reputation retire from Mudarris ul Quran, you will be provided with a pension along with assistance in starting a madrasa, InshaAllah.
  18. A maximum of two warnings will be issued for any offense, and the registration will be canceled if another offense is committed a third time.
