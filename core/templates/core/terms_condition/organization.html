Terms and conditions for institute

  1. There should be special arrangements for teachers to stay, such as separate or curtained rooms, beds, mattresses, pillows, wardrobes, etc.
  2. Meal routine must be submitted.
  3. Salary must be paid on the last working day of each month.
  4. Teachers' children should be given the opportunity to study or a minimum monthly education allowance of one thousand taka.
  5. Teachers should not be treated badly in any way.
  6. If any teacher has any serious complaint or suggestion regarding this, they should inform Alasatizah in writing. Alasatizah will inform them of their decision within a maximum of 90 days after investigation.
  7. A teacher cannot be dismissed without informing the Alasatizah, but the teacher can request a change.
  8. A maximum of three applications can be made for teacher change.
  9. If an institution changes teachers three times without reason, their registration will be cancelled.
  10. Delaying salary payments or refusing to provide any benefits is not allowed.
