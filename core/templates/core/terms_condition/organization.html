Terms and conditions for organization

  1. Salary, bonus and allowances shall be paid in the salary scale determined by the Mudarris Al-Quran.
  2. There should be special arrangements for teachers to stay, such as separate or curtained rooms, beds, mattresses, pillows, wardrobes, etc.
  3. Meal routine must be submitted.
  4. Salary must be paid on the last working day of each month.
  5. All facilities should be provided to every teacher according to the category.
  6. If it is not possible to provide accommodation for teachers who need housing, they will have to pay 100% of the rent at the village level, 70% at the district and divisional levels, and 50% at the capital level.
  7. Teachers' children should be given the opportunity to study or a minimum monthly education allowance of one thousand taka.
  8. Teachers should not be treated badly in any way.
  9. If any teacher has any serious complaint or suggestion regarding this, they should inform Mudarris Al-Quran in writing. Mudarris Al-Quran will inform them of their decision within a maximum of 90 days after investigation.
  10. A teacher cannot be dismissed without informing the Mudarris Al-Quran, but the teacher can request a change.
  11. A maximum of three applications can be made for teacher change.
  12. If an institution changes teachers three times without reason, their registration will be cancelled.
  13. Delaying salary payments or refusing to provide any benefits is not allowed.
  14. Registration must be renewed every year by paying the service charge within the specified time.
